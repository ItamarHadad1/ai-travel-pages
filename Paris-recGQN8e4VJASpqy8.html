Paris, the City of Light

Discover the beauty, romance, and history of Paris.

La Tour Eiffel

Must-see icon of Paris, and a symbol of French culture. Enjoy stunning views of the city from the top.

Musée du Louvre

One of the world's largest and most famous museums, housing an impressive collection of art and artifacts.

Cathédrale Notre-Dame de Paris

Gothic masterpiece and one of the most famous churches in the world, with stunning architecture and history.

Palais-Royal

Former royal palace turned modern art space, featuring contemporary art installations and beautiful gardens.

Champs-Élysées

Iconic avenue lined with cafes, restaurants, and high-end shops, perfect for a stylish stroll.

Montmartre

Charming historic neighborhood offering stunning views of the city, charming streets, and artistic vibe.

Palace of Versailles

Former royal palace with opulent decorations, stunning gardens, and an impressive art collection.

Stade de France

Modern stadium hosting international sports events, concerts, and cultural festivals.
