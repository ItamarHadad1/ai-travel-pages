A stunning fjord-like bay surrounded by mountains and medieval towns.

A charming walled city with narrow streets and historic architecture.

Skadar Lake

A large and peaceful lake with abundant wildlife and scenic views.

A beautiful lake on the border with North Macedonia, known for its clear waters and historic towns.

A rugged mountain range with scenic views, hiking trails, and traditional villages.

Virpazar

A small town on the shores of Skadar Lake, known for its traditional architecture and scenic views.
