Rome: The Eternal City

Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city steeped in history and culture. From ancient ruins to Baroque fountains, Rome is a must-visit destination for any traveler.

The Colosseum

One of Rome's most iconic landmarks, the Colosseum is a must-see for any history buff. Take a guided tour to learn about the gladiators who fought here thousands of years ago.

The Vatican City

A separate country within Rome, the Vatican City is home to numerous iconic landmarks, including St. Peter's Basilica and the Sistine Chapel. Be sure to dress modestly and bring a camera to capture the stunning architecture.

The Trevi Fountain

This beautiful Baroque fountain is a must-see, especially at night when it's lit up. Be sure to make a wish by tossing a coin into the water.
