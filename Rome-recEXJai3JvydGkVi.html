Rome: The Eternal City

Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city steeped in history, architecture, and art. From the ancient ruins of the Colosseum to the Vatican's stunning Sistine Chapel, Rome has something for everyone.

The Colosseum

The iconic Colosseum is one of Rome's most recognizable landmarks and a must-visit attraction. This ancient amphitheater hosted gladiator battles, public executions, and even re-enactments of famous battles.

The Vatican City

The Vatican City is home to numerous iconic landmarks, including St. Peter's Basilica, the Sistine Chapel, and the Vatican Museums. The Vatican is a treasure trove of art, history, and architecture.

Trevi Fountain

The Trevi Fountain is a beautiful baroque fountain that's a must-visit attraction in Rome. Legend has it that if you throw a coin into the fountain, you'll one day return to Rome.

Other Attractions in Rome:

  • Spanish Steps
  • Pantheon
  • Castel Sant'Angelo
  • Navona Square
  • St. Peter's Square
