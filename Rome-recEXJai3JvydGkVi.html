Rome, The Eternal City

Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city steeped in history and culture. With its rich heritage and breathtaking architecture, Rome is a must-visit destination for any traveler.

Must-see Attractions:

  • The Colosseum The Colosseum is one of Rome's most iconic landmarks and a testament to the city's rich history. This ancient amphitheater was built in the 1st century AD and could hold up to 50,000 spectators. Today, visitors can take guided tours of the Colosseum and learn about its fascinating history.
  • The Pantheon The Pantheon is a magnificently preserved ancient temple that dates back to 126 AD. This masterpiece of Roman architecture is a must-see for anyone visiting Rome. Its impressive dome and oculus make it a breathtaking sight.
  • The Trevi Fountain The Trevi Fountain is one of Rome's most beautiful baroque fountains. Legend has it that if you toss a coin into the fountain, you'll one day return to Rome. This charming fountain is a great spot to relax and take in the sights and sounds of the city.
