Explore the Eternal City

Colosseum

One of the most iconic landmarks in Rome, the Colosseum is a must-visit attraction for history buffs and architecture enthusiasts.

Vatican City

The smallest country in the world, Vatican City is home to numerous iconic landmarks, including St. Peter's Basilica and the Sistine Chapel.

Trevi Fountain

This beautiful baroque fountain is a popular spot for photos and a great place to relax and people-watch.

Spanish Steps

One of the most famous staircases in the world, the Spanish Steps are a great place to people-watch and take in the sights and sounds of Rome.

Pantheon

This ancient temple is one of the best-preserved ancient buildings in Rome and is a must-visit for history buffs.

Castel Sant'Angelo

This former fortress and mausoleum is now a museum and offers stunning views of the city from its rooftop.

Piazza Navona

This charming square is home to numerous fountains, statues, and baroque architecture, making it a great place to explore and take photos.

Roman Forum

This ancient archaeological site is home to numerous ruins and landmarks, including the Temple of Julius Caesar and the Arch of Titus.
