Explore the best attractions in Germany

Neuschwanstein Castle

A fairytale-like palace built in the 19th century, one of the most famous castles in the world.

Berlin Wall Memorial

A poignant reminder of the city's turbulent past, the Berlin Wall Memorial is a must-visit attraction.

Munich Oktoberfest

The world's largest beer festival, Oktoberfest in Munich is a time for celebration and tradition.

Museumsinsel Berlin

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this museum island is home to five world-class museums.

Rothenburg ob der Tauber

A charming medieval town with cobblestone streets, colorful half-timbered houses, and a picturesque square.

Europa-Park

One of the largest theme parks in Europe, featuring roller coasters, water rides, and live shows.

Christmas Markets

Germany's festive Christmas markets offer a magical atmosphere, traditional food, and handmade crafts.

Nuremberg Christkindlmarkt

The largest and most famous Christmas market in Germany, featuring traditional food, drinks, and handmade crafts.
