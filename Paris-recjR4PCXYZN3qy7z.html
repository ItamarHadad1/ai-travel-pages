Must-see Attractions

Eiffel Tower

A must-visit attraction in Paris, the Eiffel Tower is a iconic symbol of the city.

Louvre Museum

Home to the Mona Lisa, the Louvre Museum is one of the world's most famous museums.

Notre Dame Cathedral

A beautiful and historic cathedral, Notre Dame is a must-visit in Paris.

More Attractions to Explore

Palace of Versailles

A former royal palace, the Palace of Versailles is a stunning example of French architecture.

Montmartre

A historic neighborhood in Paris, Montmartre is known for its bohemian vibe and stunning views.

Champs-Élysées

A famous avenue in Paris, the Champs-Élysées is lined with cafes, restaurants, and shops.
