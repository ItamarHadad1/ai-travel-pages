Must-See Attractions

Eiffel Tower

A must-visit attraction in Paris, the Eiffel Tower offers stunning views of the city.

Louvre Museum

Home to the Mona Lisa and many other famous artworks, the Louvre is a world-class museum.

Notre Dame

This beautiful cathedral has been a symbol of Paris for centuries and is a must-visit attraction.

Seine River Cruise

Take a romantic cruise along the Seine River and see the city's landmarks from a different perspective.

Arc de Triomphe

This iconic monument honors the soldiers who fought and died for France and offers stunning views of the city.

Montmartre

This charming neighborhood is famous for its bohemian vibe, street artists, and stunning views of the city.

Palace of Versailles

Located just outside of Paris, this stunning palace is a must-visit attraction for anyone interested in history and architecture.

Champs-Élysées

This famous avenue is lined with upscale shops, restaurants, and cafes and offers a quintessential Parisian experience.
