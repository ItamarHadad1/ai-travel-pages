Discover Paris

The City of Light awaits you

Notre Dame Cathedral

One of the most iconic landmarks in Paris, Notre Dame is a must-visit for anyone interested in history and architecture.

The Louvre Museum

Home to the Mona Lisa and many other famous artworks, the Louvre is a must-visit for art lovers.

The Eiffel Tower

The iconic Eiffel Tower is a symbol of Paris and offers breathtaking views of the city.

Arc de Triomphe

A monumental arch honoring the soldiers who fought and died for France, the Arc de Triomphe is a must-visit for history buffs.

Pont des Arts

A pedestrian bridge with stunning views of the Seine River, the Pont des Arts is a perfect spot for a romantic stroll.

Musée d'Orsay

Home to an impressive collection of Impressionist and Post-Impressionist art, the Musée d'Orsay is a must-visit for art lovers.

Palace of Versailles

A former royal palace with stunning gardens and fountains, the Palace of Versailles is a must-visit for anyone interested in history and architecture.

Sainte-Chapelle

A beautiful Gothic chapel with stunning stained glass windows, Sainte-Chapelle is a must-visit for anyone interested in architecture and history.
