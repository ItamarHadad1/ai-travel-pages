Paris, the City of Light

Discover the beauty and romance of Paris, the capital of France.

The Eiffel Tower

A must-visit attraction in Paris, the Eiffel Tower is an iconic symbol of the city.

The Louvre Museum

Home to the Mona Lisa and many other famous artworks, the Louvre is a must-visit for art lovers.

Notre Dame Cathedral

A beautiful and historic cathedral that has been a symbol of Paris for centuries.

Palace of Versailles

A former royal palace with stunning gardens and architecture.

Montmartre

A historic and artistic neighborhood with narrow streets and charming cafes.

Champs-Élysées

A famous avenue lined with cafes, restaurants, and shops.

Arc de Triomphe

A monumental arch honoring the soldiers who fought and died for France.

Musée d'Orsay

A museum showcasing an impressive collection of Impressionist and Post-Impressionist art.
