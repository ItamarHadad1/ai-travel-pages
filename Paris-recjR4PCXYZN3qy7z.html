Paris, the City of Love

A city of romance, art, and history.

Notre Dame Cathedral

A beautiful and historic Gothic cathedral that has become one of the most iconic landmarks in Paris.

Eiffel Tower

An iron lattice tower that offers stunning views of the city and is a symbol of Paris' engineering and innovation.

The Louvre Museum

A world-renowned museum that houses an impressive collection of art and artifacts from around the world, including the Mona Lisa.

The Arc de Triomphe

A monumental arch that honors the soldiers who fought and died for France, offering stunning views of the city from its rooftop.

The Palace of Versailles

A former royal palace that is a must-visit for its opulent decorations, beautiful gardens, and historical significance.

The Sainte-Chapelle

A stunning Gothic chapel known for its intricate stained glass windows and beautiful architecture.
