Paris Travel Guide

Discover the City of Light

Eiffel Tower

A must-visit attraction in Paris, the Eiffel Tower is a symbol of the city's engineering prowess and romance.

The Louvre Museum

One of the world's largest and most famous museums, the Louvre is home to the Mona Lisa and many other artistic treasures.

Notre Dame Cathedral

A beautiful and historic cathedral that has been a symbol of Paris for over 800 years.

More Attractions

  • Arc de Triomphe

    A monumental arch that honors the soldiers who fought and died for France.

  • Palace of Versailles

    A former royal palace with opulent decorations and gardens.

  • Montmartre

    A historic neighborhood known for its bohemian vibe, street artists, and stunning views of the city.

  • Stade de France

    A modern stadium that hosts sports events and concerts.

  • Grand Palais

    A large glass and iron building that hosts art exhibitions and events.
