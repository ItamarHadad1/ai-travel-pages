Discover the City of Light

Eiffel Tower

The iconic iron lattice tower is a must-visit attraction in Paris.

Notre Dame Cathedral

This beautiful Gothic cathedral is one of the most famous landmarks in Paris.

The Louvre Museum

Home to the Mona Lisa, the Louvre is one of the world's largest and most famous museums.

Arc de Triomphe

This monumental arch honors the soldiers who fought and died for France.

Palace of Versailles

A former royal palace with opulent decorations and gardens.

Sainte-Chapelle

A stunning Gothic chapel known for its beautiful stained glass windows.

Montmartre

A historic and artistic neighborhood with a bohemian vibe.

Disneyland Paris

A theme park with Disney magic and attractions for all ages.
