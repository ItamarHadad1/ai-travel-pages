Paris Travel Guide

Discover the City of Love

Eiffel Tower

Must-visit attraction in Paris. Take the elevator to the top for breathtaking views.

Notre Dame Cathedral

A beautiful and historic cathedral that has stood the test of time. Visit the nearby Sainte-Chapelle.

Louvre Museum

One of the world's largest and most famous museums. Visit the Mona Lisa and many other masterpieces.

Palace of Versailles

A former royal palace with stunning gardens and fountain shows.

Montmartre

A charming neighborhood with narrow streets, artist studios, and stunning views of the city.

Arc de Triomphe

A monumental arch honoring the soldiers who fought and died for France.

Champs-Élysées

A famous avenue lined with cafes, theaters, and high-end boutiques.

Musée d'Orsay

A museum showcasing an impressive collection of Impressionist and Post-Impressionist art.
